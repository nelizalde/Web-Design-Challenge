Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purposes of this project was to analyze how weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To accomplish this analysis, we first pulled data from the OpenWeatherMap API to assemble a dataset on over 500 cities.

The above plot shows the general relationship between temperature and the equator/poles. As temperature increases, Latitude decreases and vice versa. Temperatures in cities closer to the equator are generally warmer and inversely, cities closer to the poles are colder.
